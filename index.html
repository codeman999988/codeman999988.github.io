Why I Love James Randi

There are lots of reason to respect, admire, and appreciate the work James has done in his life. Perhaps it would be best to put some of those reasons into a list.

  1. He was an Amazing magician.
  2. He challenged and deligitimised many grifters and charlatans who would use their deceptions to take advantage of people.
  3. He put his money where his mouth was by issuing a million dollar challenge. How cool is that?
  4. He nobly continued his mission well into his old age, and never gave up.
